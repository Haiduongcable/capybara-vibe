## 4. How It Works (Step-by-Step)

1.  **Initialize**
    `capybara init`
    *Sets up your secure configuration and learns your environment.*

2.  **Prompt**
    `capybara chat` -> "Refactor the auth module to use JWTs."
    *Just talk naturally. Capybara parses your intent.*

3.  **Watch It Think**
    *The agent drafts a plan, creates a Todo list, and starts executing edits file-by-file.*

4.  **Collaborate**
    *Interrupt, refine, or approve changes on the fly. You're always in control.*
